About Me

Hello! My name is Darius Rowser, and I’m originally from the Midwest. Growing up, I always dreamed of living in Los Angeles, drawn by its vibrant energy, opportunities, and endless innovation. After years of hard work, I finally realized that dream and moved here to pursue my passions.

I excelled in sales for most of my career, where I discovered my natural talent for building relationships and delivering results. While I loved the challenges and successes of sales, I realized I wanted to do more—particularly in the tech space. The possibilities in AI, data, and analytics fascinated me, and I began dedicating myself to learning everything I could about these fields.

Recently, I achieved a major milestone: gaining admission to a Master’s program in Applied Artificial Intelligence. This opportunity has been transformative, combining my background in sales with my growing technical expertise. My goal is to work on innovative projects that solve real-world problems while paving the way for more representation in tech.
